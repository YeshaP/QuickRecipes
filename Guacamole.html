Guacamole

Ingredients Measurements
Avacados 4 Avacados
Salt 3 Teaspoons
Lemon Juice 1 Tablespoon
  1. Smash the Avacados in a bowl.
  2. Add salt on the smashed Avacados.
  3. Add the lemon juice to the bowl.
  4. Using a spoon mix all the ingredients and serve!
